Fun Things to Do During Winter at Home

Winter is the perfect time to enjoy indoor activities! Here are some ideas to make the most of your time at home.

Board Games

Board games are a great way to have fun with family or friends. Try these:

  1. Monopoly: Buy, sell, and build your way to success.
  2. Scrabble: Create words and compete for points.
  3. Catan: Build settlements, trade, and expand your territory.

Winter Movies

Cozy up and watch these classic winter movies:

  • "Home Alone" is a classic holiday movie about 8-year-old Kevin defending his home from dim-witted burglars after being left behind during his family's vacation.
  • "Baby's Day Out" tracks Baby Bink's escapade after outsmarting his kidnappers, wandering the city, and outwitting the criminals desperate to reclaim him.

Spotify Playlist

Get into the holiday spirit by listening to cozy music while staying warm inside.

Baking

Warm up the house and indulge in delicious treats with these recipes:

Cookies

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, butter, chocolate chips.
Instructions: Preheat oven, mix ingredients, shape into cookies, bake, and enjoy!

Cake

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla extract.
Instructions: Prepare batter, bake, cool, frost, and enjoy!
